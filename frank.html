Questions

Asked before they buy.

How fast does Frank call after the sale?
Within minutes of receiving the contract. Most companies have him call while the rep is still in the home, before doubts have time to grow.
What if the homeowner doesn’t answer?
Frank leaves a friendly voicemail and retries the call at scheduled intervals. No new customer slips through.
What if he’s asked something he can’t answer?
He doesn’t guess. Frank flags the call for human follow-up and escalates by email or through your internal system.
Will homeowners know they’re talking to a bot?
Frank uses natural voice AI and polite phrasing, many homeowners assume a friendly assistant. You control exactly how he introduces himself.
What does Frank cost?
Pricing depends on call volume and features: an initial setup fee, then a cost per call. Book a strategy call and we’ll scope it for your close rate.
