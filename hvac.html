HVAC questions

Asked from the truck.

Can it handle emergency calls at 2 AM?
Yes - the agents answer 24/7 within 3 rings, with no voicemail and no queue. Urgent calls get booked straight onto your calendar, and anything that needs a human gets transferred.
What happens during a heat wave or cold snap?
Volume surges get absorbed - every call still gets a live answer while your techs run jobs. Bookings land on the calendar and your team gets a summary of every conversation.
Can it book maintenance and tune-ups?
Yes - outbound calls follow up new estimates and re-engage dormant contacts, and appointments get booked directly onto your calendar. That’s how shoulder season stays full.
Will it route calls to the right person?
It routes and triages to the right team member automatically, hands off to a human when a conversation needs one, and updates your CRM after every call.
What does it cost compared to staffing the phones?
Typically 70–90% less all-in than covering the same seats with human reps - with nights, weekends, and emergency hours included at no extra cost.
