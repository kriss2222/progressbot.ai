Questions

Asked before they buy.

How often does Ula call?
Every customer gets a check-in call once a week - and an extra call whenever something important happens, like a permit approval, an equipment delivery, or a confirmed install date.
Are the calls just “checking in”?
No - Ula is synced with your project workflow, so she knows exactly what’s happening on each job. Every call is timely and specific, not a generic touch-base.
What if a customer sounds unhappy?
She flags at-risk customers to your team early, and anything that needs a human gets handed off - so problems surface while they’re still fixable.
What does she connect to?
Your CRM, quoting tools, calendar, dialers, and financing platforms - and records update automatically after every call.
Which businesses is she built for?
Anyone who repairs, upgrades, or improves people’s homes - roofing, solar, HVAC, remodels, flooring, decks, and more. If the job has silent weeks, Ula fills them.
