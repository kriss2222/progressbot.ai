Roofer questions

Asked from the jobsite.

Can the AI actually handle roofing calls?
Yes - the live demo literally runs a roofing script. It qualifies roof age, material, property details, and timeline, then books the inspection. Before launch it gets customized to your exact script and services.
What happens during a storm surge?
The agents answer every call 24/7 within 3 rings - there’s no queue to overflow and no voicemail. Inspections get booked straight onto your calendar while your crews keep working.
Will it work with our CRM and phone system?
It works with most phone systems, VoIP platforms, and dialers, and integrates with your CRM and calendar - records update automatically after every call. Most companies are live in 1–2 weeks.
What if a caller needs a human?
The AI recognizes it, transfers the call to your team, or schedules a follow-up. Nothing gets dropped - and nothing gets guessed at.
What does it cost compared to a receptionist?
Typically 70–90% less all-in than human reps - with nights, weekends, and storm weeks covered at no extra cost.
