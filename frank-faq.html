01 What Frank does

What does Frank actually do?
Frank makes automated welcome calls - some companies call them confirmation calls - to your new solar customers: he confirms the key details of the deal, reduces cancellations, and makes sure the post-sale handoff goes smoothly.
How does Frank reduce cancellations?
By calling the homeowner quickly after the sale. Frank reinforces the decision, answers questions, and clears up any confusion about next steps - before buyer’s remorse gets a vote.
What kind of information does Frank confirm?
Homeowner name, system size, adders, financing terms, rep promises, roof condition, and next steps - like the site survey or install date.
What’s the biggest benefit of using Frank?
Fewer cancellations, cleaner handoffs, and no forgotten steps - Frank also checks that the sales rep documented every part of the agreement. It’s revenue protection.
What makes Frank different from a regular IVR or phone bot?
Frank is proactive, human-like, and built for post-sale verification - not a passive menu system. He’s trained on your data and sounds like a helpful team member.

02 The call itself

How quickly does Frank call after the sale?
Within minutes of receiving the contract. Most companies have him call while the rep is still in the home - before doubts have time to grow.
What happens if the homeowner doesn’t answer?
Frank leaves a friendly voicemail and retries the call at scheduled intervals. No new customer slips through.
What if the homeowner asks something Frank can’t answer?
He doesn’t guess. Frank flags the call for human follow-up and escalates by email or through your internal system.
Will homeowners know they’re talking to a bot?
Frank uses natural voice AI and polite phrasing - many homeowners assume a friendly assistant. You control exactly how he introduces himself.
Does Frank speak Spanish or other languages?
Yes. Frank can be trained to speak multiple languages, including Spanish, based on your customer base.
Can Frank collect additional data from the homeowner?
Yes. He can ask follow-up questions, collect details like preferred install time or insurance status, and update your system with the answers.
Can Frank send texts, emails, or transfer the call to a human?
Yes. Frank can do anything a person can do on the phone - text, email, or transfer the call - and he works with CRMs, spreadsheets, and most business software.

03 Setup & integration

Can Frank be trained on our specific sales process?
Yes - he has to be. Frank is customized for each solar company to match your terminology, your process, and your internal handoff requirements.
Can Frank be integrated with our CRM?
Yes. Frank receives deal data directly from your CRM or sales tools - no manual input needed.
How much setup is required?
A few days to a couple of weeks. We train Frank on your workflows, connect your CRM, and run test calls before launch.
Does Frank handle compliance or disclosures?
Yes. If financing, insurance, or HOA rules require specific phrases or scripts, Frank can be trained to include them on every call.
