01 The basics

What are AI sales agents?
Software that runs full sales conversations on the phone - talking, listening, answering, closing. They work 24/7, never call in sick, and never quit.
How do AI sales agents work?
Natural-language AI holds a real-time conversation: it understands the customer, handles objections, qualifies the lead, and moves the sale forward - following your process.
What types of calls can they handle?
Cold calls, inbound follow-up, appointment scheduling, lead qualification, and complete sales calls end to end.
Will customers know they’re talking to AI?
The voice is natural - pacing, tone, even filler words - and many customers never notice. Either way, every caller gets a consistent, professional experience.

02 Setup & integration

Can they integrate with our existing systems?
Yes - CRMs, calendars, and payment processors. Records update automatically after every call, so your data stays current without anyone typing.
How quickly can we get started?
Most companies are live in 1–2 weeks, including training the agent on your business and wiring the integrations.
Does it work with our phone system?
It works with most phone systems, VoIP platforms, and dialers - no rip-and-replace.
How do training and updates work?
Continuous and automatic, with no downtime. You can update scripts, pricing, and offers whenever the business changes.

03 Cost & ROI

How much do they cost compared to human reps?
Typically 70–90% less all-in. No salary, commission, overtime, or turnover - and they cover nights and weekends at no extra cost.
What ROI can we expect?
Most companies see positive ROI in 30–60 days: 3–5x more calls made, 20–40% higher conversion, at 70–90% lower cost per closed deal.
How do we measure performance?
Success and conversion rates, customer satisfaction, ROI - all in real-time analytics, call by call.

04 Control & compliance

What happens if the AI can’t handle something?
It recognizes when a conversation needs a human, transfers the call, or schedules a follow-up. Nothing gets dropped.
Is it secure and compliant?
Enterprise-grade: GDPR, CCPA, and TCPA compliant, with encrypted conversations and full audit trails.
Will this replace our sales team?
It works alongside them. The AI takes the routine volume; your reps keep the complex, high-value deals - with better information going in.
What support do we get?
24/7 technical support, plus regular performance reviews and ongoing optimization of your agents.

05 Fit & proof

What kinds of businesses benefit most?
Any business that sells by phone - especially high call volume or high rep turnover. Solar, insurance, home services, and B2B are typical.
Can they speak other languages?
Yes - the agents are multilingual, so you can serve customers in the language they’re comfortable in.
Can they handle complex sales?
Yes - multi-step processes, detailed objection handling, and real-time calculations and proposals during the call.
