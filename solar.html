Solar questions

Asked before they sign.

Can it handle solar’s longer, more complex sales?
Yes - the agents handle multi-step sales processes, detailed objections, and real-time calculations and proposals during the call, with consistent follow-up across the whole consideration window.
What about our aged lead lists?
That’s Banx’s job: following up new leads fast and re-engaging dormant ones. Companies typically report 3–5x more calls made than a human team gets through.
Can it actually reduce cancellations?
Same-day welcome calls are built for exactly that - Frank confirms the contract, financing, and rep promises minutes after signing. Solar Bear’s CEO reported cancellations dropped by about 20% after starting welcome calls.
Is outbound calling TCPA-safe?
The platform is TCPA, GDPR, and CCPA compliant, with encrypted conversations and full audit trails - and required disclosures can be built into every script.
Will it work with our CRM and dialer?
It works with most phone systems, VoIP platforms, and dialers, and integrates with your CRM and calendar - records update automatically after every call. Most companies are live in 1–2 weeks.
